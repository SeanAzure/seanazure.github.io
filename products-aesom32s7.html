Model No. AE-SOM-32S7

  • CPU: STM32H743BI up to 480MHz
  • Memory: 1MB SRAM & 8MB SDRAM
  • Storage: 2MB Flash & 8MB SPI Flash
  • Interface: 100M Ethernet, UART, USB2.0, 24bits LTDC
  • Power Supply: 5V
  • Dimensions: 45x55 mm
Description

SOM-H743 System-On-Module (SOM) is a compact (45 mm x 55 mm) mezzanine module that combines a 480 MHz Cortex-M7 STM32H743 microcontroller with 8 MB SDRAM, 16 MB NOR Flash and an Ethernet PHY buffer on a single board. The SOM interfaces to a baseboard (motherboard) using two 80-pin interface connectors.
